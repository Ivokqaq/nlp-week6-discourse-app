浅层篇章分析
▶显式篇章关系（Explicit Discourse Relation）由显式连接词
定义，通过显式连接词连接Arg1 和Arg2。在显式关系中，
Arg2 一般为句法上关联的论据，Arg1 则为另一个论据，显
式连接词由三种语法连接词产生：
1 从属连词，如because, when 等
2 并列连词，如and, or 等
3 语篇副词，如for example, instead 等
基于显式关系定义的标注样例：
(1) Third-quarter sales in Europe were exceptionally
strong[Arg1], boosted by promotional programs and new
products–although显式连接词weaker foreign currencies
reduced the company′s earnings[Arg2].
(2) Most oil companies, when they set exploration and
production budgets for this year, orecast revenue of $15
for each barrel of crude produced.
